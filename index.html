About this Dataset

This dataset comprises of 216 sequences, 3888000 frames (approx. 36 hours) collected from the a cross-lingual communication study from 36 pairs in 2 groups (each pair contains 2 people, with two kinds of group: native speaker vs. non-native speaker (EL1-EL2) and both non-native speaker (EL2-EL2)) performing 4 kinds of gestures (iconic, metaphoric, pointing and non-iconic).
